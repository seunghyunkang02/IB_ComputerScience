    C.1.8 HTML Components

    There are several html components used in social media such as title and meta tags... Title
    • Displays "Google" and the information that user has searched
    Meta Tags
    • Store the page description and the search data

    C.1.16 Structures of Web Pages

    Navigation Bar
    • White bar that allows users to go onto the pages which contain the specific area of information they have searched quickly
    • When users search information, it divides the information into images, videos and maps to allow users to get an appropriate information they need
    Banner
    • Banner of Google is placed above the search bar
    • When users search information on Google, it then shifts to the top left corner, displaying the logo of Google
    Footer
    • Allows users to click onto the information, such as Google Advertisement, Google Business and etc
